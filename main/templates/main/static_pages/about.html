About Bookr

A two-sided platform for discovering and running events.

Bookr makes it easy to attend and run events. Current event booking systems are often expensive, hard to use, can have outdated attendance info, and may cause businesses to scatter their event information across different sites.

Bookr centralises all of this — event information is clear, bookings are easily tracked, and capacity is always accurate.

For Customers

An easy, fast, and reliable way to discover and book events near you.

For Businesses

A simple system to post events, draw attendees, and manage bookings in one place.
